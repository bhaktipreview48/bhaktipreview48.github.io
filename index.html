About This Tool

ClearWeb cleans your browser experience. Many websites now fill pages with content generated by artificial intelligence. This software identifies and removes that content automatically. You see the original human-created information instead. It works to restore the web to its standard state. This tool protects your privacy and keeps your browsing simple.

✨ Why Use ClearWeb

The internet changes as AI tools become common. This software gives you control over what you see. You gain better focus because you do not have to read automated text. ClearWeb keeps your data private. It does not track your habits or your search history. You get a clean browser view every time you open a page.

ClearWeb runs on most modern Windows computers. Ensure you have one of the following web browsers installed:
